📖 How to Use Bilang

1

Quick-Start or Manual

Click a preset button above the timer, or type a client name + rate. Press Enter to start tracking.

2

Track Your Time

⏸ Pause for breaks, ✅ Done to finish and save, ✕ Cancel to end without logging billable time (session is still saved as "Cancelled" so no data is lost).

Review & Filter

Click a client name to see their history. 📊 for weekly/monthly summaries. 📥 to backup.

4

Bill Your Client
