📖 How to Use Client Clock

1

Choose Your Mode

⏱ Free Timer — track as long as you want. 🍅 Pomodoro — a time management technique where you work in focused 25-min sessions, take 5-min breaks, and after 4 sessions take a longer 15-min break. Great for staying productive without burning out. Toggle at the top.

2

Quick-Start or Manual

Click a preset button above the timer, or type a client name + rate. Press Enter to start.

3

Track Your Time

⏸ Pause for breaks, ✅ Done to finish and save, ✕ Cancel to discard. In Pomodoro with auto-stop on, the timer ends automatically — just press Done to save and start your break.

4

Review & Filter

Click a client name to see their history. 📊 for weekly/monthly summaries. 📥 to backup.
